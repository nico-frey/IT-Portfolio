How much screen-time should I allow my child per day?

Numbers vary, but technology experts and different tech manuifacturers recommend from 1h up to 3h per day.

How old does my child have to be, to own his/her own gaming device?

We recommend to not regulate the fact that your child is gaming, but rather what it's playing.
